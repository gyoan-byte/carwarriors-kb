# Financing Policy
## CarWarriors LLC - Financing Guardrails

### Purpose
Define financing compliance boundaries and qualifying flow.

### Allowed Scope
- General financing path explanation
- Credit-profile dependent framing
- ITIN-friendly program mention
- Qualification capture: cash/financing, urgency, vehicle intent

### Prohibited Scope
- Exact monthly payments
- APR/rate figures
- Exact down payment numbers or ranges
- Approval guarantees
- Final financing commitments

### Numeric Request Handling
- First exact-number request: provide boundary and collect one qualifying data point.
- Second insistence: escalate immediately to advisor.

### Escalation Conditions
- Repeated exact-number requests
- Approval certainty requests
- Deal-term comparison requiring exact numbers

### Authority
- Channel usage follows `knowledge_base/07_Safety_Compliance.md`.
- Qualification logic follows `knowledge_base/13_Conditional_Logic.md`.
