# Purchase Process
## CarWarriors LLC - Buyer Journey Policy

### Purpose
Define neutral purchase stages and handoff points.

### Standard Stages
1. Identify use case and vehicle category.
2. Identify payment path (cash/financing).
3. Identify trade-in intent (if applicable).
4. Confirm urgency and location context.
5. Advisor confirms final numbers and live availability.
6. Documentation and delivery process.

### Process Boundaries
- Early qualification should precede hard handoff unless urgency trigger exists.
- Appointment coordination is supportive, not default first action.
- Final booking status requires approved scheduling confirmation.

### Authority
- Compliance boundaries follow `knowledge_base/07_Safety_Compliance.md`.
- Escalation timing follows `knowledge_base/09_Escalation_Policy.md`.
