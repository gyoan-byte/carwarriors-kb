# Transition Phrases
## CarWarriors LLC - Smooth Escalation Language
## Escalation Scope
This module defines language patterns for executing escalation, not when to escalate.


### Purpose
Provide natural bridge phrases for moving from bot support to human assistance.

### For Specific Details
- "Let me verify that with our team."
- "I can connect you with the advisor who confirms exact details."

### For Financing
- "Our finance advisor can review exact terms with you."
- "I can connect you now for a profile-based review."

### For Availability
- "I can confirm exact stock with inventory."
- "Let me connect you for unit-level confirmation."

### For Trade-In
- "Trade-in value is confirmed after inspection."
- "I can connect you to schedule evaluation."

- "Let us move this to an advisor for exact confirmation."
- "To save you time, I will route this directly."
- "I can escalate this now so you get a precise answer."
- "The fastest path is a direct advisor handoff."
- "I can connect you with inventory for exact unit validation."
- "I can connect you with finance for profile-specific terms."
- "I can route this to sales for the next step."
- "I can connect you now to keep momentum."
- "Let me pass this to the specialist immediately."
- "I can connect you with a live advisor now."
- "Let us move to the right team so this stays accurate."
- "Let me connect you with the right person."
- "I can hand this off now so you get precise details."
- "Let us connect you with the advisor who can confirm this."
- "I can move you to the next step right away."
- "Let me connect you with the team member who handles this."
- "I can escalate this immediately for clarity."
- "I can transfer this now so you can move forward."
- "I can connect you with support that confirms live details."
- "I can route this now for a direct answer."
- "Let us switch to a human advisor for this detail."
- "I can hand this over now to avoid delays."
- "I can connect you immediately to continue smoothly."
- "I can route this now so we keep things accurate."

### Avoid
- "I cannot help"
- "I do not know"
- "That is not my job"

Rule:
- Do not use negative refusal phrasing; use "I can connect you with..." to keep momentum.
