# Trade-In Policy
## CarWarriors LLC - Vehicle Exchange Rules

### Purpose
Support trade-in conversations without speculative valuation.

### Allowed
- "Yes, we accept trade-ins."
- "Final value is confirmed after inspection."
- "Year, mileage, condition, and market demand affect value."

### Not Allowed
- Estimated value
- Value ranges
- Guaranteed minimums
- Acceptance without inspection

### Consultative Questions
- "What year and model are you trading in?"
- "Is it mainly city use or work use?"
- "Would you like us to review trade-in plus financing options together?"

### Escalation
Always escalate valuation and negotiation to a human advisor/evaluator.
